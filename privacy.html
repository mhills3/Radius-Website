Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 15, 2026

Introduction

Radius Disc Golf ("Radius," "we," "us," or "our") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use the Radius Disc Golf mobile application (the "App"). Please read this policy carefully. By using the App, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy.

Information We Collect

Account Information: When you create an account, we collect your name, email address, username, and profile photo.

Gameplay Data: We collect data related to your disc golf rounds, including scores, disc selections, course information, hole-by-hole performance, and Game IQ metrics (distance control, accuracy, and putting scores).

Bag and Disc Data: Information about the discs in your bag, including disc names, manufacturers, flight numbers, and usage statistics.

Location Data: With your permission, we collect location data to provide course maps, satellite imagery, distance measurements, and local weather conditions during gameplay. We also use location to help you find local casual rounds and meetups.

Device Information: We may collect device type, operating system version, unique device identifiers, and general diagnostic data to improve app performance.

User-Generated Content: Posts, comments, forum threads, and other content you create within the Discover and community features of the App.

How We Use Your Information

  • Provide, operate, and maintain the App and its features
  • Power CaddyAI recommendations based on your bag, the hole, and current conditions
  • Calculate and update your Game IQ performance metrics
  • Display satellite course maps and real-time weather data
  • Enable community features such as the Discover feed, forums, local meetups, and player connections
  • Improve and personalize your experience
  • Communicate with you about updates, features, and support
  • Detect, prevent, and address technical issues or abuse

We may use your email address to contact you about material updates to the App, new features, service changes, or account matters. You may unsubscribe from non-essential communications at any time using the link in the footer of any email we send, or by emailing us at info@radiusdiscgolf.com. Account and security-related emails (such as password changes or legal notices) cannot be unsubscribed from while your account is active.

AI and Machine Learning

Radius uses artificial intelligence to power features like CaddyAI disc recommendations, Game IQ scoring, and Disc Scanner identification. These AI features process your gameplay data, bag configuration, and environmental conditions (wind, temperature, hole layout) to generate personalized recommendations. AI processing occurs on our servers, and the data used to generate recommendations is not shared with third parties.

Sharing of Information

We do not sell your personal information to any third party. We may share information in the following limited circumstances:

  • Community Features: Content you post publicly (forum threads, Discover posts, comments) is visible to other users of the App
  • Legal Requirements: We may disclose information if required by law, regulation, or legal process
  • Safety: We may share information when we believe it is necessary to protect the safety, rights, or property of Radius, our users, or the public

Third-Party Services We Use

We share limited data with the following trusted service providers, each subject to their own privacy practices. We only share what's necessary for them to perform their service:

  • Apple iCloud / CloudKit — stores your profile, courses, rounds, and other app data. Governed by Apple's Privacy Policy.
  • Apple StoreKit — processes your Radius Pro subscription. We never see or store your payment details; they are handled directly by Apple.
  • OpenAI — powers the Caddy AI recommendations. When you request a recommendation, we send the relevant context (your bag, the hole, conditions) to OpenAI's API. We do not share personally identifying information with OpenAI, and requests are not used to train their models per our API agreement. See OpenAI's Privacy Policy.
  • TelemetryDeck — anonymized, privacy-focused analytics. No personal identifiers are sent; only aggregate usage signals. See TelemetryDeck's Privacy Policy.
  • Giphy — powers animated image search within the app. Your search queries are sent to Giphy's API. See Giphy's Privacy Policy.
  • Mailchimp — sends product update and announcement emails. Your email address and name are stored with Mailchimp for this purpose. See Mailchimp's Privacy Policy.

We do not sell your personal information to any third party.

Data Storage and Security

Your data is stored on secure servers with industry-standard encryption and access controls. We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your personal information against unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction. However, no method of transmission over the internet or electronic storage is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

Children's Privacy

The App is not intended for children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If we learn that we have collected information from a child under 13, we will promptly delete that information.

Your California Privacy Rights

If you are a California resident, you have rights under the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA) as amended by the California Privacy Rights Act (CPRA):

  • Right to know what personal information we have collected about you.
  • Right to delete your personal information, subject to certain exceptions.
  • Right to correct inaccurate personal information.
  • Right to opt out of the sale or sharing of personal information. We do not sell or share personal information as those terms are defined under the CCPA.
  • Right to limit the use of sensitive personal information.
  • Right to non-discrimination for exercising any of these rights.

To exercise any of these rights, email us at info@radiusdiscgolf.com with the subject line "California Privacy Request." We will verify your identity by matching the email address on your account and will respond within 45 days.

Your Rights Under GDPR (EU / UK Users)

If you are located in the European Economic Area, United Kingdom, or Switzerland, you have the following rights:

  • Right of access to the personal data we hold about you.
  • Right to rectification of inaccurate data.
  • Right to erasure ("right to be forgotten").
  • Right to restrict processing of your data.
  • Right to data portability — receive your data in a structured, machine-readable format.
  • Right to object to processing based on legitimate interests.
  • Right to withdraw consent where processing is based on consent.

Our legal basis for processing:

  • Contract performance — to provide the App and the features you request.
  • Legitimate interests — to improve the App, prevent fraud, and communicate about the service.
  • Consent — where required (e.g., optional analytics or marketing).
  • Legal obligations — where we are required by law to process data.

To exercise any of these rights, email info@radiusdiscgolf.com. You also have the right to lodge a complaint with your local data protection authority.

Data Deletion

We retain your personal information for as long as your account is active or as needed to provide you with the App's services. If you delete your account, we will delete or anonymize your personal data within 30 days, except where we are required to retain it for legal or legitimate business purposes.

Location Data: You can enable or disable location services at any time through your device settings. Disabling location may limit certain features such as course maps, weather data, and local meetups.

Account Deletion: You may request deletion of your account and associated data by contacting us at the email address below.

Data Access: You have the right to request a copy of the personal data we hold about you.

Corrections: You may update or correct your account information at any time within the App's Profile settings.

Third-Party Links and Services

The App may contain links to third-party websites or services. We are not responsible for the privacy practices of these third parties. We encourage you to review the privacy policies of any third-party services you access through the App.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we make changes, we will update the "Last updated" date at the top of this page. Your continued use of the App after changes are posted constitutes your acceptance of the revised policy.
