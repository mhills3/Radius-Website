How It Works

Three steps inside.

1

Join the community

Hop into the Discord. Meet other players, follow updates, and find your way around the Radius community.

2

Submit the courses you've built for review. Our team approves each one before it counts toward your total.

Earn your access

Hit 5 approved courses for Inner Circle. Hit 10 for Inner Circle Pro and a full year of Radius Pro on us.
