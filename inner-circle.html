The Inner Circle

Built by the players
who build the game.

The Inner Circle is our home for the disc golfers helping shape Radius from the ground up — a place for early access, exclusive drops, and a real seat at the table.

Join the Community See the path

For our founding members.

Radius isn't just an app — it's a community. The players who help us map courses, test what's next, and grow this thing aren't just members. They're founders. And founders get a voice in where Radius goes next.

The Path

How you get in.

Everyone starts in the community. From there, the players who put in the work to map courses earn their way into the Inner Circle.

5 Courses

Inner Circle

Map 5 approved courses to earn your spot.
  • Founding member status
  • Early access to beta features
  • First look at limited drops
  • Exclusive offers from partners
  • Private channels with the team
  • A vote on what we build next
10 Courses

Inner Circle Pro

Map 10 approved courses to unlock the top tier.
  • Everything in Inner Circle
  • Radius Pro free for a full year
  • Direct line to the founders
  • Top-tier recognition in the community
  • First access to everything new
How It Works

Three steps to founding member.

1

Join the community

Hop into the Discord. Meet other players, follow updates, and find your way around.

2

Map courses

Submit the courses you've built for review. Our team approves each one before it counts toward your total.

3

Earn your access

Hit 5 approved courses for Inner Circle. Hit 10 for Inner Circle Pro and a year of Radius Pro on us.
